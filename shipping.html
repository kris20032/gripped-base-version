Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package's journey to your doorstep. If you don't receive tracking information within 2 business days of placing your order, please contact our support team.

Returns & Exchanges

Our Returns Policy

We want you to love your gripped. socks. If for any reason you're not completely satisfied, you can return unworn, unwashed items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

30-day return window from delivery date
Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging
Original tags must be attached
Refunds processed within 5-7 business days of receiving return

How to Return

1
Contact Us

Email us at returns@gripped.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll send you a prepaid return label within 24 hours.

2
Pack Your Items

Place items in their original packaging (if available) with tags attached. Include your packing slip or order confirmation.

3
Ship It Back

Attach the prepaid label to your package and drop it off at any authorized carrier location. Save your tracking number for your records.

4
Get Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? We're happy to help! Contact us at exchanges@gripped.com with your order number and what you'd like to exchange for. We'll send you the new item right away and include a prepaid return label for the original item.

Note: Exchanges are subject to availability. If your preferred item is out of stock, we'll process a full refund instead.

Questions About Your Order?

Our customer service team is here to help. Reach out with any questions about shipping, returns, or your order status.
