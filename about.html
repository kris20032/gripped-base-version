About gripped.

We believe that every step matters. Founded on the principle that exceptional grip shouldn't compromise comfort, gripped. creates premium socks that keep you stable, confident, and grounded.

Our Mission

At gripped., we're on a mission to revolutionize how people move. Whether you're practicing yoga, training at the gym, or simply moving through your day, our grip socks provide the stability and confidence you need to perform at your best.

We combine premium materials with innovative grip technology to create socks that don't just meet expectations—they exceed them. Every pair is designed with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring maximum comfort, durability, and performance.

Our Values

Quality First

We never compromise on quality. Every material is carefully sourced and every product rigorously tested to ensure it meets our exacting standards.

Sustainable Practice

We're committed to minimizing our environmental impact through responsible sourcing, efficient production, and sustainable packaging solutions.

Customer Focus

Your satisfaction drives everything we do. From design to delivery, we prioritize your experience and stand behind every product we create.

Our Story

gripped. was born from a simple frustration: finding grip socks that actually worked without sacrificing comfort or style. After countless hours of research, testing, and refinement, we created our first pair—and knew we had something special.

What started in a small studio has grown into a global brand trusted by athletes, yoga enthusiasts, and everyday movers around the world. But despite our growth, our core commitment remains unchanged: creating the best grip socks possible.

Today, every pair of gripped. socks represents our ongoing dedication to innovation, quality, and the simple belief that better grip leads to better movement.
